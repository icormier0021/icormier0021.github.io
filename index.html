About

Cormier Research Consulting is led by Isaac Cormier, BSc (Hons), a research and clinical-neuropsychology professional with experience across psychology, health research, psychometrics, and statistical programming.

Isaac’s work sits at the intersection of behavioural science, clinical research, data analysis, and research methods. His consulting focuses on helping researchers move from messy data and vague analysis questions toward clear, reproducible, and interpretable outputs.

Common projects include R/Python analysis workflows, SPSS support, data cleaning, research tables and figures, survey analysis, cognitive and behavioural research support, and preparation of results for manuscripts, presentations, or reports.
